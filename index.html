Recipe #1 - Tortila chips

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Maize Flour 1 cup
Wheat Flour 1/2 cup
Turmeric 1/4 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Oil 2 tsp
Water As much as required
Oil As much as required

Step 1: Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take 1 cup maize flour and ½ cup wheat flour.
Step 2: Also add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp pepper, ½ tsp salt and 2 tsp oil.
Step 2: Mix well making sure all the spices are combined well.
Step 4: Now add ½ cup water and start to knead the dough.
Step 5: Knead to stiff and smooth dough adding water as required.
Step 6: Pinch a ball sized dough, and dust with wheat flour.
Step 7: Roll slightly thick, dusting wheat flour as required.
Step 8: Prick the roll with a fork, preventing from puffing up while frying.
Step 9: Trim the sides and cut into triangle pieces. Now drop the triangle chips into the medium hot oil. or bake in preheated oven at 180 degree celcius for 25 minutes.
Step 10: Fry on low to medium flame stirring in between until the nachos chips turn crisp and golden brown for 10 minutes. Step 11: Drain off the chips over kitchen paper.

CAN BE STORED FOR 1 MONTH

Recipe #2 - Strawberry pudding

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 cup
Cream 3/4 cup
Gelatin/Agar Agar 2 tsp
Powdered Sugar 1 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Salt 1 pinch
Warm water 2 tsp
Strawberry 2 cups

Step 1: Firstly, prepare milk mixture by heating 1 cup milk and ¾ cup cream.
Step 2: Whisk and mix well making sure the cream combines well with milk.
Step 3: Now add 1 tsp agar agar and whisk well.
Step 4: Cook the milk on medium-low heat till it steams (do not boil). Step 5: Further, add ½ cup powdered sugar and dissolve sugar.
Step 6: Turn off the flame and add 1 tsp vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
Step 7: Whisk and mix well. Let the milk cool for 10 minutes before pouring to the glass.
Step 8: Furthermore, place the serving glass in a bowl so that it gets tilted slightly.
Step 9: Pour in prepared milk mixture till it reaches the rim.
Step 10: Now refrigerate it for 2 hours or overnight, till it sets completely.
Step 11: After 2 hours, make sure to check the milk mixture has set completely.
Step 12: Now prepare strawberry mixture 1 tsp of agar-agar/gelatin in 2 tbsp warm water.
Step 13: Mix well and dissolve agar-agar completely.
Step 14: Further, add 2 cups of strawberry puree and mix well making sure the pulp gets mixed up with agar-agar uniformly. Step 15: Pour the strawberry mixture into the glass. Step 16: Refrigerate it for 2 hours or overnight, till it sets completely.

ENJOY THE STRAWBERRY PUDDING!

Recipe #3 - Ginger tulsi tea

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1 cup
Bay leaves 2
Basil/ tulsi 5 - 6
Ginger 1 inch
Cinnamon 1 inch
Cardomom 3 pods
Pepper 1 tsp
Cloves 1/2 tsp
Honey 1 tsp

Step 1: Firstly, in a large saucepan take 4 cup water.
Step 2: Add 2 bay leaf, few tulsi, 1 inch ginger, 1 inch cinnamon, 3 pod cardamom, 1 tsp pepper and ½ tsp cloves.
Step 3: Stir well and boil for 5 minutes or until flavours are absorbed well.
Step 4: Strain the tea and add 1 tsp honey. You can alternatively use jaggery while boiling.

GINGER TULSI TEA IS READY TO SERVE
